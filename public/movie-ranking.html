The Best Movies According to Diana

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Cloud Atlas

  • The actors/actresses play multiple characters with prosthetics and special makeup effects.
  • I refuse to believe the original plot.
  • This movie made my list because it is too philosophical to pass.

The Lord of the Rings Trilogy

  • If I can binge-watch these movies in one sitting, I'm winning in life.
  • These movies take you on such a long journey that you can't help to find solutions for Frodo to get rid of the Ring faster. How about those eagle, anyone...?

Pride and Prejudice

  • This is my ultimate comfort movie that I always watch once a year.
  • The musical scores in this film is the soundtrack to my life if it had one.
  • I love an epic romantic speech.
